About Us

Dig It Up Ground Works SW Ltd is a trusted groundwork and labour supply company delivering reliable services across Essex, Suffolk and surrounding areas.


We specialise in all aspects of groundwork, from site clearance and excavation through to foundations, drainage, and external works. With a strong understanding of construction requirements and on-site efficiency, we ensure every project is completed safely, on time, and to a high standard.


Alongside our groundwork services, we also provide experienced labour to the construction industry. Whether you require a full gang or individual operatives, we supply skilled workers who are fully certified and ready to integrate into your project.
